About Sri Gayatri Jr College

Sri Gayatri Jr College, established in the heart of Rajampet, stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Andhra Pradesh. With a rich legacy of nurturing young minds, our institution has been committed to providing quality intermediate education for over two decades.


Our college is renowned for its academic rigor, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art infrastructure. We believe in holistic development of students, combining academic excellence with character building, leadership skills, and moral values. Our mission is to prepare students not just for higher education but for life itself.


Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to excel in their chosen fields and contribute positively to society.


Why Choose Sri Gayatri Jr College?

Courses Offered

MPC

Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry

Perfect for students aspiring for Engineering, Architecture, and Physical Sciences. Comprehensive curriculum covering advanced mathematics, physics concepts, and chemistry fundamentals.

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Mechanics & Thermodynamics
  • Organic & Inorganic Chemistry
  • Problem-solving techniques

BiPC

Biology, Physics, Chemistry

Ideal for medical and life sciences aspirants. Detailed study of biological processes, physics applications in medicine, and chemistry of life processes.

  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Botany & Zoology
  • Medical Physics
  • Biochemistry

CEC

Commerce, Economics, Civics

Designed for future business leaders and commerce professionals. Focus on business principles, economic theories, and civic responsibilities.

  • Business Studies
  • Microeconomics & Macroeconomics
  • Political Science
  • Accounting Principles
